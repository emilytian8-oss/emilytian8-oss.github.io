About Yuda Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.

Yuda Electronic Technology Co., Ltd. is a professional printed circuit board (PCB) manufacturer located in Dongguan, Guangdong Province, China. Formerly YUDA ELECTRONIC LIMITED (Yostar PCB), the company was renamed in 2026.

Over the years, Yuda has grown into a stable and reliable PCB supplier with around 500 employees and a production area of about 8,000 m². We are dedicated to providing cost-effective and high-quality PCB fabrication services for customers in different industries, including consumer electronics, communication, industrial control, power electronics and more. With rich manufacturing experience and strict quality control, we aim to be your long-term and trustworthy PCB partner in China.
