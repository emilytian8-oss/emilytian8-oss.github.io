About Yuda PCB

Dongguan Yuda PCB Co., Ltd is a professional printed circuit board (PCB) manufacturer located in Dongguan, Guangdong Province, China. Founded in 2015, Yuda PCB has grown into a stable and reliable PCB supplier with around 500 employees and a production area of about 8,000 m².

We are dedicated to providing cost-effective and high-quality PCB fabrication services for customers in different industries, including consumer electronics, communication, industrial control, power electronics and more. With rich manufacturing experience and strict quality control, we aim to be your long-term and trustworthy PCB partner in China.
