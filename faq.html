Frequently Asked Questions / 常见问题

Below are typical questions about different PCB types. Each Q&A is provided in both English and Chinese. 如下是关于不同类型 PCB 的常见问题，每条问答都提供英文和中文版本。

1. Multilayer PCB 多层电路板

Q1: What information do you need to quote and manufacture a multilayer PCB? / 多层 PCB 报价和生产需要提供哪些资料？ +

EN: For multilayer PCBs, please provide at least the following information:

  • Gerber files or ODB++ data, including outline, drill file and all copper layers.
  • Layer count and stack-up (layer order, dielectric thickness, reference planes).
  • Finished board thickness and finished copper weight (inner/outer layers).
  • Material type and Tg (standard FR‑4, high‑Tg, halogen‑free, etc.).
  • Surface finish (HASL, lead‑free HASL, ENIG, OSP, etc.).
  • Solder mask and silkscreen color.
  • Quantity, delivery requirement and any special test or reliability requirement.

CN: 对于多层 PCB，请至少提供以下资料：

  • Gerber 或 ODB++ 数据，包括板框、钻孔文件以及所有铜层文件。
  • 层数及叠层结构（层序、介质厚度、参考平面等）。
  • 成品板厚及成品铜厚（内层/外层）。
  • 板材类型及 Tg 要求（普通 FR‑4、高 Tg、无卤等）。
  • 表面处理方式（有铅/无铅喷锡、沉金、OSP 等）。
  • 阻焊颜色及字符颜色。
  • 订单数量、交期要求，以及是否有电性/可靠性等特殊测试要求。

Q2: What is your typical capability range for multilayer PCBs? / 你们多层板的常规工艺能力范围是多少？ +

EN: Our typical multilayer PCB capability range includes:

  • Layer count: 4–40 layers (higher layer counts require engineering evaluation).
  • Board thickness: around 0.6–8.0 mm (depending on stack-up and layer count).
  • Finished copper: usually 1 oz, up to 2 oz or thicker upon request.
  • Min. trace/space: typically 4/4 mil, smaller design on request after review.
  • Min. mechanical drill: about 0.20 mm (0.15 mm possible for some designs).
  • Surface finishes: HASL, lead‑free HASL, ENIG, OSP, etc.

CN: 我们多层板的常规工艺能力范围大致如下：

  • 层数：4–40 层（更高层数需工程评估）。
  • 板厚：约 0.6–8.0 mm（视叠层和层数而定）。
  • 成品铜厚：常规 1 oz，可根据要求做到 2 oz 或更高。
  • 最小线宽/线距：通常 4/4 mil，更小线宽/线距需评估后确认。
  • 最小机械钻孔：约 0.20 mm（部分设计可做到 0.15 mm）。
  • 表面处理：有铅/无铅喷锡、沉金、OSP 等。

Q3: How do you ensure the quality and reliability of multilayer PCBs? / 你们如何保证多层 PCB 的品质和可靠性？ +

EN: We ensure multilayer PCB quality through:

  • Strict incoming material control and approved vendor list (AVL).
  • Process control for inner layer imaging, etching, lamination and drilling.
  • AOI inspection for inner and outer layers where applicable.
  • 100% electrical test for batch orders.
  • Final visual inspection and reliability checks when required.

  • 内/外层 AOI 检测（视设计情况而定）。

2. HDI PCB 高密度互连板

Q6: What HDI structures and microvia capabilities can Yuda PCB support? / 裕达 PCB 能支持哪些 HDI 结构和微盲孔能力？ +

EN: We can support common HDI build‑up structures, including:

  • 1+N+1, 2+N+2, up to 3+N+3 HDI constructions.
  • Blind vias, buried vias and via‑in‑pad structures.
  • Staggered and stacked microvias (stacking levels subject to evaluation).

CN: 我们可支持的常见 HDI 结构包括：

  • 1+N+1、2+N+2，最高可到 3+N+3 等 HDI 构造。
  • 盲孔、埋孔以及焊盘中孔（via‑in‑pad）结构。
  • 错位叠孔和同轴叠孔（具体叠层层数需工程评估）。

EN: For HDI capability details, contact sales@yudapcb.com or call +86 135 3804 3239.
CN: HDI 工艺能力详情请联系 sales@yudapcb.com 或拨打 +86 135 3804 3239。

Q7: How should I specify HDI structures and microvias in my design files? / 在设计文件中应如何标注 HDI 结构和微盲孔？ +

EN: Please clearly indicate in your stack-up drawing and fabrication notes:

  • Which layers are connected by blind vias and buried vias (e.g. L1–L2 blind, L2–L3 blind, L3–L6 buried).
  • Whether microvias are staggered or stacked, and how many levels of stacking.
  • Any via‑in‑pad locations on BGAs or fine‑pitch components.
  • Minimum via diameter, capture pad size and annular ring requirements.

CN: 请在叠层图和制板说明中清晰标注：

  • 各层之间的盲/埋孔关系（如 L1–L2 盲孔、L2–L3 盲孔、L3–L6 埋孔等）。
  • 微盲孔是错位叠孔还是同轴叠孔，以及叠孔层数。
  • 所有 BGA 焊盘中孔（via‑in‑pad）及细间距器件区域。
  • 最小孔径、焊盘尺寸及环宽要求。

EN: If you are not sure how to mark HDI structures, email your draft to sales@yudapcb.com for guidance.
CN: 如不确定如何标注 HDI 结构，可将草图发送至 sales@yudapcb.com 获取指导。

Q8: What should I pay attention to when designing HDI PCBs to improve yield and reliability? / HDI PCB 设计时应注意哪些方面以提高良率和可靠性？ +

EN: We recommend that you:

  • Avoid unnecessary stacking of microvias and keep stack levels reasonable.
  • Ensure sufficient pad size and annular ring for microvias.
  • Balance copper density between different areas to reduce warpage.
  • Keep high‑density BGA areas as clean and clear as possible in the design.

CN: 建议在 HDI 设计中注意：

  • 避免过度叠孔，控制微盲孔叠孔层数在合理范围内。
