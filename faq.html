Multilayer PCB 多层电路板

Q1: What information do you need to quote a multilayer PCB? 多层 PCB 报价需要提供哪些资料？ +

EN: For multilayer PCB quotation, please provide your complete Gerber files and the following key parameters:

HDI PCB 高密度互连板

Q2: What HDI structures and microvia capabilities can you support? 你们可以支持哪些 HDI 叠构和微盲孔能力？ +

EN: We support common HDI build-up structures such as 1+N+1, 2+N+2 and up to 3+N+3, including blind vias, buried vias and via-in-pad designs. Typical laser microvias are 0.10–0.15 mm finished, with staggered or stacked configurations depending on your reliability requirements. Please clearly indicate via structures and layer connections in your stack-up drawing.

CN: 我们可实现常见 HDI 叠构，例如 1+N+1、2+N+2，最高可到 3+N+3，支持盲孔、埋孔以及焊盘中孔（via in pad）等结构。常规激光微孔成品孔径约 0.10–0.15 mm，可根据可靠性要求设计为错位叠孔或同轴叠孔。请在叠层图中清晰标注各层的盲/埋孔关系及孔结构。

High-Frequency PCB 高频电路板

Q3: How do I choose materials and stack-up for high-frequency PCB designs? 高频 / 高速 PCB 应该如何选择材料和叠层结构？ +

EN: Material and stack-up choice depend on operating frequency, loss budget and cost. We can work with high-frequency laminates such as Rogers and other low-loss materials, as well as hybrid FR‑4 structures. When consulting us, please provide your frequency range, target impedance, loss requirements and any preferred materials so that we can recommend a suitable stack-up.

Rigid-Flex PCB 刚挠结合板

Q4: What should I pay attention to when designing rigid-flex PCBs? 设计刚挠结合 PCB 时需要注意哪些设计要点？ +

EN: For rigid-flex designs, please clearly define rigid and flex areas, avoid vias in dynamic bending zones, route traces along the bending direction where possible, and provide mechanical drawings for complex folding structures. Indicate coverlay openings, stiffener materials and thicknesses in your drawings for accurate manufacturing.

CN: 刚挠结合设计中，应在图纸中清晰区分刚性区与柔性区，避免在反复弯折区域放置过孔和焊盘；走线尽量沿弯折方向布线并避免直角转折；对于复杂折叠结构，建议提供 2D/3D 机械图。还需在图纸中标明 Coverlay 开窗、加强板材质及厚度，以便准确制造。

Blind & Buried Via PCB 盲埋孔电路板

Q5: When should I use blind and buried vias and what details are needed? 什么时候需要采用盲孔 / 埋孔结构？需要在资料中说明哪些细节？ +

EN: Blind and buried vias are used in high-density designs to save surface space and improve routing. Please specify which layers are connected by blind and buried vias, whether vias are stacked or staggered, and mark any via-in-pad areas. Providing clear via structures in the stack-up drawing helps us evaluate manufacturability and cost.

CN: 在高密度设计中，为节省表面空间、提高布线自由度，通常会采用盲孔和埋孔结构。请在叠层图中标明各盲孔 / 埋孔的层间关系（如 L1–L2 盲孔、L3–L6 埋孔等），说明是否存在叠孔（stacked）或错位孔（staggered），并在图纸中清晰标注 BGA 焊盘中孔等关键区域，以便我们评估可制造性和成本。

Impedance-Controlled PCB 阻抗控制电路板

Q6: What data do you need for accurate impedance control on multilayer PCBs? 多层阻抗板要实现精准阻抗控制，需要客户提供哪些数据？ +

EN: We need an impedance table or drawing listing target values (single-ended/differential), related layers, and initial line width/spacing. A proposed stack-up with material type and dielectric thickness is also required. We will use our impedance tools to fine-tune trace geometry and can provide impedance coupons and test reports if needed.

CN: 为了实现精确的阻抗控制，我们需要您提供阻抗表或阻抗图，注明各阻抗线（单端 / 差分）的目标阻抗值、所在层和初始线宽 / 线距。同时需要提供拟采用的叠层结构（材料类型、介质厚度等）。我们会通过阻抗仿真工具适当调整线宽线距，并可按照要求提供阻抗测试 Coupon 和测试报告。

Hybrid-Lamination PCB 混合压合电路板

Q7: What is a hybrid-lamination PCB and when is it recommended? 什么是混合压合 PCB？在什么情况下建议使用？ +

EN: A hybrid-lamination PCB combines different laminates in one stack-up, for example FR‑4 with high-frequency materials or metal-core sections. It is recommended when part of the circuit requires special electrical or thermal performance while other areas can use standard materials, allowing you to balance cost and performance.

CN: 混合压合 PCB 是指在同一块板的叠层中同时使用不同类型的基材，例如在关键高频区域使用高频材料，在其他区域使用 FR‑4，或在局部功率区引入金属芯 / 厚铜层。这种方案适用于局部性能要求较高（如高频、散热）的场合，可以在满足性能的前提下有效控制整体成本。
