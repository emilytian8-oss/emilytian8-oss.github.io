Multilayer PCB — Details

Typical: 4–10 layers standard; up to 40 layers possible with engineering review. Surface finishes: ENIG, HASL, OSP.

HDI PCB — Details

Support microvias, buried/blind vias, laser via drilling, line/space down to design rules. 3rd-order HDI supported on request.

High-Frequency PCB — Details

Materials: Rogers, PTFE, Taconic, etc. Controlled dielectric constant and low-loss materials for RF applications.

Rigid-Flex / Hybrid — Details

Single-sided flex, multi-layer rigid-flex, coverlay and thermal management available; design guidance provided.

Blind / Buried Vias — Details

Blind and buried via drilling, via filling and stacked via processes supported for compact designs.

Impedance Control — Details

Impedance calculations and test reports, tolerance per request (typical ±8% or tighter by agreement).

Custom & Hybrid Laminates — Details

Special laminates, thick copper, metal-core PCBs and mixed-material stackups available upon consultation.
