PCB Products

Yuda PCB provides a wide range of PCB products similar to mainstream Dongguan PCB manufacturers, covering standard FR-4 PCBs, multilayer boards and quick-turn prototypes.

Standard FR-4 PCBs

General purpose PCBs for consumer electronics, power supplies, chargers and more.

  • Single-sided and double-sided PCBs
  • Common board thickness and copper thickness
  • Green solder mask as standard, other colors available
  • Suitable for a wide range of applications

Multilayer PCBs

Multilayer FR-4 PCBs for communication, control and other complex systems.

  • 4–8 layer standard multilayer PCBs (higher on request)
  • Stack-up design support and impedance control
  • Fine line and via capability for high-density designs
  • Stable performance for signal integrity

Prototype & Small Batch PCBs

Fast and flexible prototype and small batch service helps you verify design and enter the market quicker.

  • Quick response quotation and engineering review
  • Flexible order quantity, suitable for R&D projects
  • Short lead time for urgent orders
  • Same technology base as volume production

OEM/ODM PCB Manufacturing

We support OEM/ODM customers and long-term cooperation with distributors and traders, including stable production and continuous quality improvement.
