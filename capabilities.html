Manufacturing Capabilities

Yuda PCB has manufacturing capabilities similar to mainstream Dongguan PCB factories, covering standard FR-4 PCBs and multilayer boards. The table below shows our general capability range. For special requirements, please contact us for confirmation.

Technical Capability Overview

Item Capability Remarks
Layer count 1–8 layers (higher on request) Standard FR-4 multilayer PCBs
Board size Max. panel size around 610 × 510 mm Custom panelization available
Board thickness 0.6–2.0 mm (typical) Other thickness can be discussed
Copper thickness 1 oz standard, up to 2 oz Heavier copper on request
Min. trace / space 4 / 4 mil (0.10 / 0.10 mm) Smaller design may be evaluated
Min. finished hole 0.20 mm Via in pad and microvias on request
Surface finish HASL, Lead-free HASL, ENIG Other finishes can be discussed
Solder mask color Green (standard), red, blue, black, white Special colors require confirmation
Silkscreen color White, black Other colors on request
Testing 100% electrical test for batch orders Flying probe or fixture test

Quality & Process Control

  • Incoming material inspection and supplier control
  • Process monitoring and inspection for each key step
  • AOI inspection for inner and outer layers (if applicable)
  • 100% electrical test for volume orders
  • Final visual inspection and packaging

We continuously improve our production process and quality system to provide stable and reliable PCBs for long-term cooperation customers.
